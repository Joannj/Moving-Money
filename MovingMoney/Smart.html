The Key to achieving your goals is setting S.M.A.R.T goals...

S - Specific - Be Specific about what your goals are ask Who? what

M - Measure - You must be able to measure what you have achieved with your goal with something tangible. Happiness cant be measured, but a new wadrobe in a smaller size can.

A - Attainable - Ask yourself is your goal achievable? Is the results of this goal really worth the time and effort you are going to put in?

R - Relevant - Is your goal relevent you to? Or do you want to achieve the specific goal because it is what is accepted in society or what your friends and family want for you?

T - Timely - Time is money! Get a realistic deadline that suits your pocket for yourself to ensure that you are not spending 6 months on a 1 month goal. Procrastinating leads to Cancellation!
