Distinguish NEEDS, WANTS

A vital skill needed when saving money is distinguishing what you need and what you want.

NEED:

Require (something) because it is essential or very important rather than just desirable
- A roof over your head
- Enough food and water to maintain your health
- Basic health care and hygiene products
- Clothing (just what you need to remain comfortable and appropriately dressed)
Quite simply, the economic definition of a need is something needed to survive. In economics, the idea of survival is real, meaning someone would die without their needs being met.

WANT
Have a desire to possess or do (something); wish for.
SO, if its not on the needs list above, IT'S A WANT! Dont confuse the two.
